CAREER OPPORTUNITIES: WELCOME ABOARD

We are committed to provide our Employees a stable work environment with equal opportunity for learning and personal growth. Creativity and innovation are encouraged for improving the effectiveness of Peach Airways. Above all, Employees will be provided the same concern, respect, and caring attitude within the organization that they are expected to share externally with every Customer.


Aircraft Maintenance & Engineering

Maintenance Operations Center Analyst

Conduct analysis of large, complex, and/or technical problems and translate into viable solutions. Ensure solutions are delivered ontime and within budget. Closely monitor Maintenance Operations Center departmental performance.

Material Specialist

A Material Specialist Employee will do all work generally recognized as the routine duties of a Material Specialist and/or stockroom employee in and about the Company storerooms and stockrooms, including but not limited to receiving, shipping, checking, classifying, issuing, inventorying (on a nonexclusive basis), storing and warehousing of supplies, tools, materials and the operation of stores equipment and the preparation and maintenance of records and reports in connection therewith.

Regulatory Affairs Investigator

Responsible for conducting investigations of events or concerns related to regulatory compliance for Southwest Airlines Technical Operations, and preparing written reports on the events. Acts as a liaison between Southwest Airlines Technical Operations and the Federal Aviation Administration on all regulatory matters.

Airport Operators

Customer Service Agent

Provides legendary Customer service by handling ticketing, baggage check-in, baggage claims, reservations, information inquiries and resolving complaints and problems.

Ramp Agent

Ramp Agents provide legendary Customer service by handling cargo and baggage service, directing and servicing aircraft and related activities on the airport ramp. Handles all aspects of loading, stowing and unloading baggage, cargo airmail, air freight, ballast and company materials according to a predetermined plan received either electronically or manually from an Operations Agent.

Operations Agent

An Operations Agent provides exemplary Customer Service by assuring weight and balance of aircraft, efficient boarding of aircraft and coordinating Ramp, Operations, Provisioning and aircraft servicing to accomplish on-time performance.

Corporate Careers

Technology Category Manager

Leads sourcing events and negotiations and manages procurements for highly visible and high dollar contracts with the goal of protecting the interest of Southwest Airlines. Mentors other Team Members and advocates continuous improvement efforts.

Facilities Maint Tech

The Facilities Maintenance Senior Field Technician I will perform on-call repairs and preventive maintenance of the facilities and equipment as assigned by the Employee's Leaders.

Fuel Category Manager

Coordinates the scheduling and planning of fuel supply chain management and operations, conducts spend and market analysis to coordinate supply chain strategy development. Leads complex negotiations with suppliers utilizing strong background in fundamental sourcing skills and experience

Marketing & Corporate Sales

Business Consultant Performance

The Performance Business Consultant is ultimately tasked with working with corporate sales leadersto drive revenue by drawing upon data analysis, cross-functional collaboration, and knowledge to provide actionable insights and meaningful trend analysis.

Customer Representative

Provides Customer Service by effectively communicating with Customers via telephone and electronic channels (including but not limited to social media). Customer Representatives must efficiently and properly resolve Customer concerns and promote the airline's message by providing information, booking reservations, selling tickets and performing related tasks.

Business Consultant Sales Analytics

The Business Consultant assists Corporate Sales Team by helping to determine which Customers/accounts to target, review portfolio statistical performance and behavior/market trending. They also analyze data in order to spot sales trends and perform Customer and offering analysis.

Technology

System Engineer

Responsible for vendor management, partner governance, and oversight to deploy systems which are complete, designed and managed over life cycles. (Technical solutions) research problems, plan solutions, recommend software and systems, and coordinate developments in an orderly manner to meet business requirements. Including but not limited to: monitor and test performance and capacity for potential bottlenecks, identify possible solutions, and work with partners to implement fixes.

Technical Analyst

Effectively applies knowledge and skills of analysis, business processes, tools, domains, project methodologies, requirements definitions, and testing to solve a range of problems. Identifies problems, performs root cause analysis, investigates information, performs impact analysis, and formulates and executes plans to develop solutions. Identifies strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions, or approaches to problems.

Software Engineer

Design, write program code, test, document, and implement functionally appropriately, technically sound, and well-integrated application systems in accordance with Customer requirements, and organizational methodologies and standards. Respond to production problems and implement immediate resolution efforts across technology areas.
